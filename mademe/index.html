Make Be Leave: A Happening at Harwood

Details

Join DRY MTN for a collaborative performance celebration. You will be led through a series of training exercises to prepare you for distributing our publication to the greater community. We will screen print, fold, staple and trim our third issue together. We will celebrate in creation, discussion and destruction. We will make, be and then leave holding piles of handmade art from the heart.

FAMILY FRIENDLY. ALL AGES. ALL ARE WELCOME.

Thursday, August 31st from 6pm – 8pm
